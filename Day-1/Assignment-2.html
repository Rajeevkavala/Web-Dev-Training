My Favorite Movies

Name: C/o Kancharapalem

Genre: Drama

Kancharapalem is a 2018 Telugu-language film directed by Venkatesh Maha.
It is a romantic drama that tells the story of love and relationships
in the Kancharapalem locality of Visakhapatnam, India.

Name: Jersey

Genre: Drama , Sport

Jersey is a 2019 Telugu-language sports drama film directed by Gowtam Tinnanuri.
The film follows the journey of a former cricketer who returns to the sport in his 30s to fulfill his son's wish.

Name: Baahubali 2: The Conclusion

Genre: Action, Adventure, Drama

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian epic action film directed by S.S. Rajamouli.
It is the sequel to Baahubali: The Beginning and continues the story of the royal family of Mahishmati.

Name: Hi Nanna

Genre: Drama, Romance

Hi Nanna is a 2023 Telugu-language film directed by Vikram Kumar.
The film explores the relationship between a father and his daughter, highlighting the emotional bond they share.

Name: RRR

Genre: Action, Drama, History

RRR is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language epic action drama film directed by S.S. Rajamouli.
The film is set in the 1920s and follows the fictionalized lives of two legendary revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem.
